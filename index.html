About the Podcast

All Things Git is the podcast about Git. We talk to the people who build Git, who create tools for it and who teach people how to use it. We bring you a new episode every two weeks, discussing the Git version control system with a guest. Subscribe to the podcast on our RSS, on iTunes or SoundCloud. And be sure to follow us on Twitter and Facebook.
